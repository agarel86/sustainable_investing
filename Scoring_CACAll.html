Explore raw LSEG ESG datapoints by company, organised by the official 10‑category / 3‑pillar structure.

1. How to use this page

  • Company selection: Use the dropdown below to pick a firm; the ESG datapoints and all rankings update automatically.
  • Datapoints & definitions: The datapoints table lists only numeric and Boolean variables with at least one non‑missing value across firms. Click “Definition” to see the LSEG description, unit and polarity.
  • Distribution & scores (section 3): Click any datapoint row to select it. The ranking table shows companies ordered by ESG performance on that variable, with scores from 0–100 (adjusted for polarity in the definitions). Choose “Best in universe” or “Best in industry (by GICS sector)”.
  • Industry averages (section 3b): For the selected datapoint, see the average score per GICS sector and how many firms have data.
  • Category-level scores (section 4): Category scores aggregate the datapoint scores within each ESG category. Switch between simple mean and materiality‑weighted mean; the “ESG score” column is the industry‑specific materiality‑weighted average of category scores.
