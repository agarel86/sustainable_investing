Values are approximate country-level estimates based on authoritative datasets: Global Carbon Project, WHO Air Quality Database, Jambeck et al. (2015) on ocean plastics, IUCN Red List, and Global Forest Watch.

Some values are interpolated or estimated from published ranges. Data should be treated as indicative rather than exact for all country-year combinations.
